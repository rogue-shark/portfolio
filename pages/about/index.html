About Me

Hola! My name is Shantanu. I enjoy building things that come to life on the vast canvas of the internet.

Although I'm fairly new in this field (considering the wide scope of things), but my journey into web development actually started way back when I was in school. I still remember the first time I was introduced to web development (unaware at the time) — when I made a website for my school by editing HTML and CSS of a template I downloaded from some website. Ah, those were the days!
Fast forward to the present, and I'm at it again — programming and building things. My path to get here was a bit winding. I actually studied electrical engineering, but then the pandemic hit in my final year, and like many others, I found myself at a crossroads. It was during this time that I rediscovered my love for programming and building things — just with one tool at my disposal — a laptop! And so, I decided to take the plunge and pursue web development as a full-time career. It's been a wild ride so far, and I'm excited to see where this journey takes me.

In the grand scheme of things, coding feels like building order out of chaos — a practice where logic meets creativity to shape something meaningful. And isn’t that what life is about? Finding structure in the randomness and creating something that matters (especially to oneself (always starts with the self) )
